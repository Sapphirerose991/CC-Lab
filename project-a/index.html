[ Imaginary Nautilus ]

Scientific Name:

Nautilus belauensis imaginary

Discovered by / at:

Time traveller, Another parallel universe

Family:

They are part of the Nautilus family who survived mass extinctions from ancient time...

Habitat:

A mosaic ocean with all the creatures in it are in geometric shape.

Appearance:

Their appearances are extremely free. By interacting with you, their shapes changed. If you stay in one position, they are the usual spirals.

Key Characteristics:

  • Beautiful geometric spirals
  • Floats to ocean top at night and sinks to bottom at day.
  • Will chase the direction of sun and moon
  • Die when the maxium capacity of the sea is reached

What we know so far about Imaginary Nautilus?
Imaginary Nautilus is the decedants of ancient giant nautilus living in a parallel space. They vary in size but obey the same clock-wise spiral as their ancestors did. In Ordovician, the world was covered by warm ocean, and ancient Nautilus became the greatest predators of their time. They have straight, curved, and spiral shapes. Today, only a small family of Nautilus survived in the warm Indian and Pacific ocean. In a parallel space, the decedants of ancient giant nautilus still exists. We call them Imaginary Nautilus. They live in the mosaic ocean where every creature is in geometric space, preys in deep ocean at day time and floats to sea surface at night. They chase the light of sun and moon, for nevigating in the ocean. When an Imaginary Nautilus died, its color will fade, but its body will not completely fade away. The shell turned to almost transparent, but do not disappear. If you watch close enough, you will be able to spot these “ghosts” of them.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature
To summon an Imaginary Nautilus, you need to press the mouse for a few seconds. The shape of nautilus shell will depend on you mouse trails. If you don’t press long enough time, they will be not willing to strech their heads out. You can control day and nigh, direction of Sun and Moon, by pressing “⬆️⬇️⬅️➡️“ keys on your keyboard, and watch the Imaginary Nautilus chasing lights. After reaching maximum capacity of the habitat, the oldest nautilus died. It slowly fades away. Be careful, if you want to summon a new Nautilus.
